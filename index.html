Overview

Shop groceries based on your selected recipe! Check your inventory and buy only what is needed saving money, time and no excess buy . You can use SmartPantry to scan hand written recipe notes or import recipes from all over the web in just a few taps into your mobile phone, tablet or PC! Smart Pantry tracks your frequently shopped list to help you order quickly. Checks the inventory based on your selected recipe and shows you the missing ingedients for you to order.

Use all of your favorite recipes to quickly generate grocery lists and meal plans for yourself or the entire family! Hubby on the way home from work and you didnt have time to tell him what to pickup? No problem, just setup sync on his device, and he can have the list of items in almost real time! Like sharing your recipes on Social Media? Smart Pantry allows you to quickly post your favorite recipes directly to your Facebook, Twitter or Pinterest accounts.
